Course Description

Are you looking to improve your English? This course is designed for everyone who want to learn practical English skills. This is aimed for people from Marathi medium to master their English skills. The course covers all four language areas: listening, speaking, reading, and writing. It also includes grammar, vocabulary building, and soft skills training. You can choose the modules that fit your needs, whether you want to improve your communication for work or studies or for general conversations. Each lesson is short and easy to understand, with explanations in English & Marathi. There are videos, exercises, quizzes, and a course book with transcripts and extra materials. The course is completely online and self-paced, so you can learn at your own convenience. This course is designed to be extremely comprehensive and convenient with more than 150 lessons each varying between 3 minutes to 16 minutes, so you can finally stop searching for different resources and focus on improving your English - systematically.
